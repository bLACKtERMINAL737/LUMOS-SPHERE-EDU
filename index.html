Visa Requirements

Key Requirements for Top Destinations

A quick overview of what you'll need. Our counselors guide you through every detail specific to your situation.

🇬🇧 UK Student Visa

  • Unconditional offer from a UK university
  • IELTS 5.5–7.0 (program dependent)
  • Proof of funds (9 months tuition + living)
  • UKVI CAS number from your university
  • Immigration Health Surcharge payment
  • Biometric Residence Permit (BRP)

🇨🇦 Canada Study Permit

  • Acceptance from a Designated Learning Institution
  • IELTS 6.0–7.0 or TOEFL 80+
  • Proof of funds (CAD 10,000+ per year)
  • Clean criminal record certificate
  • Biometrics registration
  • Statement of purpose for study

🇦🇺 Australia Visa 500

  • Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE)
  • IELTS 5.5–7.5 (institution dependent)
  • Genuine Temporary Entrant (GTE) statement
  • Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC)
  • Financial capacity evidence
  • Biometrics and medical check

🇩🇪 Germany Student Visa

  • University admission letter
  • German B1/B2 or English proficiency
  • Blocked account (€10,332/year)
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Proof of accommodation in Germany
  • Certified academic transcript translation

🇲🇾 Malaysia Student Pass

  • Offer from MOHE-approved university
  • English proficiency proof
  • Valid passport (18+ months)
  • Medical check at approved clinic
  • EMGS student visa application
  • Proof of financial support

🇨🇳 China X1/X2 Visa

  • JW201/JW202 admission notice
  • HSK language proficiency (or English)
  • Physical examination record
  • No criminal record certificate
  • Financial guarantee letter
  • Completed visa application form
FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about studying abroad with Lumos Sphere Edu.

What is the cost of Lumos Sphere Edu's services?+
Our initial consultation is completely free. Service fees vary depending on the level of support and your chosen destination. We offer transparent, flexible packages — contact us for a personalized quote with no hidden charges.
When should I start my application process?+
We recommend starting at least 12–18 months before your intended intake date. This allows adequate time for test preparation (IELTS/TOEFL), document collection, application submission, and visa processing.
Do I need IELTS to study abroad?+
Most English-medium universities require IELTS, TOEFL, or PTE scores. However, some institutions accept internal English tests or waive requirements for students from English-medium schools. Our counselors will advise based on your specific profile and destination.
Can you help me get a scholarship?+
Absolutely. We have a dedicated scholarship team that identifies merit-based, need-based, and destination-specific scholarships. We've helped students secure funding ranging from partial tuition waivers to full scholarships covering living expenses.
What if my visa application gets rejected?+
Our 98% visa success rate speaks for itself, but in rare rejection cases we analyze the reason, strengthen the application, and reapply at no additional service charge. We never abandon students mid-journey.
Do you provide support after I arrive at university?+
Yes. Our post-arrival support includes help with enrollment, SIM card registration, bank account setup, local orientation, and student community connections. Our WhatsApp support line remains active for 6 months post-arrival.
